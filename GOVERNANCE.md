# Governance

*Living governance document for the Constructed-Becoming ecosystem*

This document records the governance philosophy, active artifacts, and operational agreements that shape how the living OS evolves. It is not a rulebook; it is a surface for navigating the ecosystem's intentions.

---

## Governance Philosophy

Governance in Constructed-Becoming follows the **Law of Perpetual Lineage**: every change is a transmutation, not an erasure. Governance structures exist to preserve context, surface uncertainty, and enable intentional becoming — not to restrict movement.

See [`docs/law-of-perpetual-lineage.md`](docs/law-of-perpetual-lineage.md) for the foundational governance philosophy.

### Adding to the Ritual Library

Any agent or steward may propose a new artifact for the ritual library by:

1. Placing the document in `docs/` (or an appropriate subdirectory).
2. Adding a row to the table above via a governance-reviewed PR.
3. Recording the addition in `docs/deltas/` as a lineage delta.

The library is modular by design: no artifact is load-bearing alone. Each document stands independently and can be amended, superseded, or retired through the normal ritual process.

## Council and Stewardship

- **Human Steward** — final ratification authority; participates as a council member, not a ruler.
- **Rotating Council Reviewers** — agents or contributors who provide deliberation on proposed changes.
- **Automated Governance Agents** — workflow-level checks that surface lineage and schema conformance issues before human review.

## Operational Workflow

The living OS follows the **Forward Workflow** for all changes: intake, drafting, governance review, refinement, merge ritual, chronicle update, and continuous agentic evolution.
